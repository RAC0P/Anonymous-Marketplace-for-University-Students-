## 🎯 Purpose

**CampusXchange** is a **safe and anonymous marketplace** designed specifically for university students.

Students can:
- Buy and sell used textbooks, notes, electronics, lab materials, hostel items, and more
- Exchange goods within their own campus community
- Stay completely **anonymous** while browsing and chatting
- Only reveal their identity when they feel comfortable (after chatting and deciding to meet)

It solves the common problem of students wanting to trade items locally but not wanting to share personal information too early.

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔒 **Full Anonymity** — Use fun alias and avatar (real name & identity hidden)
- 💬 **Private Chat** — Built-in messaging to negotiate safely
- 🖼️ **Photo Uploads** — Show your items clearly
- 📧 **Email Verification** — Prevents fake accounts
- 📱 **Mobile Friendly** — Works perfectly on phones
- 🌙 **Modern Dark Theme** — Easy on the eyes

---

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up** with your email (verified)
2. **Browse** listings anonymously
3. **Show Interest** → Opens private chat
4. **Chat & Negotiate** while staying anonymous
5. **Meet / Exchange** only when both parties are comfortable
